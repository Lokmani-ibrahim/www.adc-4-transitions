Our USPs

Innovative Methods for Mastering Communication in Foreign Languages
Through our experience and the feedback from our clients, we understand that holding a language certificate—regardless of the candidate's proficiency level—is not sufficient to guarantee effective communication. Many candidates, despite their language certification, often struggle to convey their ideas clearly and adapt to diverse cultural contexts. To address these challenges, AdC employs innovative methods that differentiate us from traditional approaches. While we integrate international training materials, our unique techniques go beyond conventional methods. We focus on helping candidates master communication in a foreign language through practical, real-world interactions. Our customized strategies not only enhance linguistic skills but also foster cultural adaptation and confidence, ensuring that our candidates can engage effectively and succeed in any global professional environment.
Integration Training
At AdC, we understand that relocating to a new country involves more than just professional skills—it’s about adapting to a new environment and culture. That’s why we offer comprehensive integration training alongside our other professional development programs. Our tailored training helps candidates quickly adapt to their new surroundings by focusing on cultural norms, local customs, and effective communication strategies. This ensures a smooth transition, enabling individuals to embrace their new environment confidently and integrate successfully, both professionally and personally, into their new country.
Tailored Skills Assessments and Career Orientation
At AdC, we conduct comprehensive skills assessments to align our candidates' abilities with market demands and their career aspirations. By understanding their strengths and goals, we provide personalized professional guidance and support, helping individuals navigate the path to their dream jobs. This ensures that each candidate receives targeted orientation, enhancing their career prospects and ensuring they are well-prepared for the opportunities that match their skills and ambitions.
Free Job Contracts
At AdC, we provide free job contracts abroad, helping candidates secure rewarding international career opportunities. Our dedicated support guides you every step of the way, ensuring a smooth transition into global employment.
Comprehensive Candidate Evaluation for Informed Decision-Making
At AdC, we provide our clients with detailed "Skills Assessment" reports, offering a thorough analysis of candidates' progress and observations throughout their training and coaching periods. These assessments are scientifically designed to provide an accurate and objective overview of both technical competencies and soft skills, highlighting the strengths, areas for improvement, and overall development of each candidate. By tracking these aspects over time, our "Skills Assessments" give clients a clear understanding of a candidate’s potential in the context of their role. This data-driven approach helps our clients make well-informed decisions, ensuring they select candidates whose skills and profiles are a strong fit for the job at hand, while also identifying potential areas for further development. Ultimately, these assessments enhance the recruitment process by providing a clear, measurable understanding of a candidate's readiness for the professional environment.
Providing Candidates with Mastery in Four Languages
AdC provides candidates who, due to their diverse backgrounds, are proficient in at least four languages— German, English, French, and Arabic. This multilingual proficiency enhances communication skills, making candidates versatile professionals who can seize opportunities across diverse markets and build successful global caree.
120-160 Hours of Language Training
At AdC, we offer intensive language training programs spanning 120 to 160 hours per proficiency level, from A1 to C1. Our courses are available in German, English, French, Spanish, and Italian, ensuring comprehensive language development. Through these structured programs, we empower candidates to achieve fluency, mastering both linguistic skills and cultural nuances necessary for success in a global professional environment. Our language training programs and methods are designed to significantly increase candidates' chances of obtaining language certificates in accordance with CEFR standards.

AdC in Brief
AdC is a professional development center offering language and technical training, along with soft skills development to help individuals succeed in the workplace. We also assist businesses in finding qualified employees who meet their specific skill requirements.
What sets us apart?
At AdC, we address a key challenge in today’s workforce: candidates may possess the necessary qualifications but struggle to communicate effectively in their acquired languages. Our unique approach focuses on enhancing communication skills through tailored methods and integration training for the countries candidates are moving to. We offer professional development training programs that cover soft, hard, and technical skills, ensuring alignment with the demands of the VUCA (Volatile, Uncertain, Complex, Ambiguous) world. Additionally, we provide skills assessments to guide each individual's growth. Our training includes 120-160 hours per level to ensure proficiency( From A1 to C1).
What are the class schedules?
AdC is dedicated to providing candidates with the flexibility they require in order to learn the language. We offer a range of courses, including both face-to-face and online options, as well as a variety of timetables, including mornings, afternoons, and evenings. This allows students to select the option that best suits their schedule.
What languages do we offer ?
At AdC, we understand that effective communication is the key to success in today's interconnected world. That’s why we offer language training in German, English, French, Spanish, and Italian—languages that open doors to diverse cultures, industries, and professional opportunities worldwide. Our language programs are designed not only to teach theoretical language knowledge, but to develop strong communication skills that are essential for thriving in a globalized work environment (through AdC’s innovative methods). We provide 120-160 hours per proficiency level, ensuring our learners gain the confidence and fluency they need to communicate effectively across various settings— whether it's engaging in professional discussions, writing emails, or negotiating with international partners.
What is a bespoke training program, and how can it benefit your company?
A bespoke training program is a tailored learning experience designed specifically to meet the unique needs of your company and its employees. Unlike standard off-the-shelf training programs, bespoke programs are customized to address your organization's specific goals, challenges, and industry requirements. By working closely with you, we identify skill gaps, desired outcomes, and the areas where your team needs the most support. This allows us to create a program that aligns with your company's vision and helps employees develop the right skills for their roles. Whether it’s leadership development, technical skills, or soft skills, bespoke training ensures that the content is relevant, engaging, and immediately applicable.

About AdC

At AdC, we specialize in empowering both individuals and organizations through sustainable development and professional growth. Since our establishment in 2011, we have been dedicated to delivering innovative project management solutions across various fields, including Automotive and Railway, all aimed at creating a lasting impact on both local and global scales.
Our two core services HM-Consulting and Hallo Forma work in synergy to build essential skills and open valuable opportunities across a wide range of industries:

  • HM-Consulting: partners with international organizations to manage sustainable development projects, focusing on areas such as vocational training programs and the introduction of dual training systems in Tunisia. Our work helps both public and private sectors adapt to rapidly changing needs, ensuring long-term growth and resilience in evolving markets.

  • Hallo Forma: offers a comprehensive suite of professional training programs designed to elevate individual capabilities. We provide courses in languages such as German, English, Spanish, and French, alongside technical certifications in specialized fields like welding, mechanical design, electrical, finance and water management. Our aim is to help individuals not only grow their expertise but also enhance their careers by equipping them with practical, industry-relevant skills.

Through our integrated approach, AdC bridges skills gaps, fosters international connections, and creates lasting opportunities for both individuals and businesses alike. Our commitment to continuous development, innovation, and collaboration ensures that we remain at the forefront of empowering individuals and transforming industries across the world.
