FAQ

What is AdC ?
AdC is a global service provider founded in 2011, specializing in sustainable development, project management, and professional training. We focus on empowering individuals and organizations through innovative solutions, helping them succeed in a rapidly evolving world.
How can I contact ADC ?
You can reach us through our contact form on the website, or by emailing us directly at contact@adctransitions.com. You can also find our office location on Google Maps by clicking on the Find us button or you can reach us on our number +216 29 142 006.
Who can benefit from our services?
Our services are designed for individuals looking to advance their careers, organizations seeking to implement sustainable projects, and businesses in need of skilled talent. Whether you’re a young professional, a company, or a government organization, ADC for Transition offers tailored solutions to meet your needs.
Do we offer services internationally?
Yes, we operate internationally. Our projects, training programs, and recruitment services are designed to support clients and partners across different regions and industries worldwide.
What types of training programs do we offer?
At Hallo Forma, we provide a range of training programs, including:
  • Languages Training: German, English, Spanish, and French.
  • Technical Training: Welding, mechanical design, water management, electrical , accounting, and finance.
  • Career Guidance: Coaching and support for individuals entering the job market or seeking career development.
What makes AdC unique in preparing candidates for professional success?
AdC prepares candidates for success by offering hands-on training, real-world experience, and global job opportunities. Our Hallo Forma programs not only teach practical skills but also focus on enhancing communication skills, which are crucial in today’s job market. We understand that many people struggle with communication, and we address this through targeted training.
Our courses are designed to provide 120-160 hours of training per proficiency level, ensuring that candidates gain the depth and confidence needed for career growth. Additionally, we offer training that combines learning with practical experience, as we helps connecting candidates with job opportunities worldwide.
With a focus on sustainability, adaptability, and effective communication, AdC ensures candidates are ready to excel in today’s fast-paced and ever-changing job market.
How do I register for a training program?
To register for one of our training programs, simply visit Our Trainings section on our website. Select the course you're interested in and follow the registration instructions. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact us, and our team will guide you through the process.
What is the process for international Professional Placement through AdC?
AdC helps young professionals find job opportunities abroad in cooperation with our partners. After you submit your application, we will evaluate your qualifications, match you with potential employers, and assist with the professional placement process. Our team offers personalized support, including career coaching and advice on working abroad. In addition, we also assist companies in finding the appropriate candidates who meet their specific requirements, ensuring a perfect match for both the organization and the candidate.
What is the "Chancenkarte" and how can AdC guide me in pursuing it?
The Chancenkarte (Opportunity Card) is a program designed to provide individuals with the opportunity to live and work in Germany, offering various benefits such as access to the labor market and social services. At AdC, we assist candidates in applying for the Chancenkarte by providing essential support, including language training, technical training, and help with file processing and file recognition. While we do not directly issue the Chancenkarte, our services are designed to guide and support individuals through the application process, ensuring they meet the necessary requirements for a successful application.
Do we offer recognition of diplomas at AdC?
At AdC, we provide comprehensive assistance to help you through the process. Our team supports you in gathering and preparing all necessary documentation, ensuring that everything is in order for submission. Additionally, we offer guidance and advice to help you navigate the steps required to obtain official recognition of your diploma in Germany. Our goal is to make the recognition process and application as smooth and efficient as possible for you.
How do our courses help improve communication skills, especially in a time when effective communication is a growing challenge ?
At AdC, we understand that even with a B2 level in a language, many individuals still struggle to communicate effectively. That’s why we focus on providing innovative learning methods that specifically address this gap. We recognize that strong communication skills are essential for success in today’s fast-paced, interconnected world. Our courses are designed to enhance both verbal and written communication, focusing on clarity, confidence, and effectiveness. We provide practical exercises and real-world scenarios that help trainees improve their ability to express themselves, actively listen, and adapt to different communication styles. Given that many people today face challenges in effective communication, our training equips individuals with the tools needed to excel in both professional and personal interactions, fostering better relationships and career opportunities.
What is the "Ausbildung" and how does AdC prepare candidates for this type of program?
The "Ausbildung" is a vocational training program in Germany that combines on-the-job training with theoretical education. It is designed to equip individuals with the practical skills and knowledge needed to succeed in various professions. At AdC, we prepare our candidates to align with the requirements of the Ausbildung by providing comprehensive training in three key areas:
  1. Language Training: We offer language courses in German to ensure our candidates can communicate effectively in the workplace and understand the theoretical components of the Ausbildung program. Proficiency in German is essential for success in these programs.
  2. Technical Training: Our technical training courses are tailored to provide candidates with the skills required in specific industries. This hands-on training ensures that candidates are well-prepared for the practical aspects of the Ausbildung.
  3. Soft Skills Training: In addition to language and technical skills, we focus on developing essential soft skills, such as teamwork, problem-solving, and communication. These skills are crucial for integrating into the professional environment and succeeding in the Ausbildung.
  4. Integration Training: At AdC, we provide integration training to help individuals adapt to the culture, customs, and professional norms of the country they are migrating to, ensuring a smooth transition and successful integration.
Through this approach, AdC ensures that candidates are fully prepared to take on an Ausbildung and thrive in their chosen career paths.
